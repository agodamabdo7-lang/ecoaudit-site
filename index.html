How EcoAudit works

The app combines a simple questionnaire with engineering‑grade reference data. In three steps you get a clear view on your biggest sources of waste and what to do first.

1. Describe your site

Enter just the essentials: floor area, location, main equipment and last energy bills. No need for detailed CAD drawings.

2. Model energy flows

A calculation engine estimates loads for lighting, HVAC, hot water and plug loads using standard profiles and climate data.

3. Get actions & savings

The tool ranks measures by impact and cost: from “free behavior changes” to “CAPEX with short payback”.
